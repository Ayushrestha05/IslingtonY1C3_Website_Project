Most Helpful Websites

W3Schools is a web developers site, with tutorials and references on web development languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, Python, jQuery, Java, C++, C#, React, XML, W3.CSS, and Bootstrap, covering most aspects of web programming. W3Schools was originally created in 1998 by Redsnes Data, a Norwegian software development and consulting company.

I accessed W3Schools when I was confused on certain kind of code. W3Schools also helped a lot in adding new functionalities.



Stack Overflow is a question and answer site for professional and enthusiast programmers. It is a privately held website, the flagship site of the Stack Exchange Network. It features questions and answers on a wide range of topics in computer programming. This website serves as a platform for users to ask and answer questions.

Whenever I had problem that couldn't be solved, I asked questions in StackOverflow. The StackOverflow community is very quick and responsive with answers. That is why I visit StackOverflow whenever a problem couldn't be solved by myself.
